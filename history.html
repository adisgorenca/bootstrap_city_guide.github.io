Facts about Anchorage

Anchorage is the largest city in alaska, the city and the surrounding towns contains more than 50% of the entire state’s population. It is the only large city in alaska which isn’t economically based on fishing or mining, it was originally based on the alaskan railroad. It later on centered on military activities, and is now centered on oil drilling.

It was hit by the second largest earthquake in recorded history in 1964, the earthquake was a magnitude 9.2. It killed 115 people and cost .7 billion dollars in today’s money.
