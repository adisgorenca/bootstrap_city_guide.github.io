Attractions in Anchorage

There is a very large variety of activities to do during the Summer. You can visit one of the five national parks around Anchorage. The Denali National Park is shown on the left, where you can observe wildlife like caribou, brown and black bears, and moose. You can also observe one of the 60 glaciers near Anchorage. You can also visit Chugach State Park where you can do many activities such as hiking, rafting, biking, kayaking, fishing, etc.

There is a very large variety of activities to do during the Winter. There are countless hiking trails and the Northern Lights can be clearly viewed. There are also many lakes for ice fishing such as Beach, Campbell Point, Clunie, Delong, Mirror, Sand, Goose and Jewel. Fish like Arctic char, grayling, pike and landlocked salmon can be caught. You can also go skiing during the 150 day skiing season.
